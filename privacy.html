PointMe Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Overview

PointMe values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

Local Device Data

PointMe stores user activity, arrival history, and preferences locally on your device. We do not operate a central server that stores your personal data.

Location Data

The App may request access to your device location to enable arrival and venue features. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to PointMe servers.

Third-Party API Requests

When retrieving venue information (such as names or details), the App may make requests directly to third-party services (e.g., mapping providers). These requests may transmit basic technical information such as IP address as part of standard internet communication.

3. Data Storage

All user-generated data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, your locally stored data will also be deleted.

4. Data Sharing

PointMe does not sell, rent, or share personal data. We do not operate analytics trackers or advertising networks.

5. Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for securing your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

PointMe is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of changes.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: pointme.app@gmail.com

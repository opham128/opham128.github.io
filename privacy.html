PointMe Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Overview

PointMe is designed to operate without user accounts and without maintaining centralized user databases. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed and how it is handled.

2. Information Processed

a. Location Data

The App requests access to precise location data in order to enable venue detection and arrival functionality. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored by PointMe on external servers.

b. Local Device Storage

Arrival history, preferences, and activity data are stored locally on your device. PointMe does not operate servers that collect or store this data. Deleting the App will remove locally stored information.

c. Third-Party API Requests

The App queries Mapbox services to retrieve mapping and venue information. When such requests are made, limited technical data (such as IP address, device type, and request metadata) may be transmitted directly to Mapbox as part of standard internet communication. PointMe does not control how third-party providers process data collected through their services.

3. Data Collection by PointMe

PointMe does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information on its own servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not use advertising networks. We do not use cross-app tracking technologies.

5. Data Retention

Because PointMe does not maintain user accounts or central databases, data remains on your device unless you delete the App.

6. Security

Data stored locally is subject to the security protections of your device. Users are responsible for securing their own devices.

7. Children’s Privacy

PointMe is not intended for children under 13.

8. California Privacy Rights

Because PointMe does not collect or store personal information on centralized servers, there is no personal data to access, delete, or modify beyond what is stored locally on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any updates.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: pointme.app@gmail.com
